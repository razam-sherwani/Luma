How Recommendations Are Generated

Method 1: Cluster Network Analysis

Our AI analyzes patient clusters across the network to identify patterns and successful treatment outcomes:

  • Patient Clustering: Groups patients with similar demographics, conditions, and treatment histories
  • Outcome Analysis: Identifies which treatments have been most successful for similar patient groups
  • Network Insights: Leverages data from healthcare providers with similar patient populations
  • Predictive Modeling: Uses machine learning to predict treatment effectiveness based on cluster data

Method 2: Web Scraping & Research Analysis

Our system analyzes the selected doctor's patient data and finds relevant medical research:

  • Patient Data Analysis: Parses through all patients under the selected healthcare provider
  • Condition Identification: Identifies common illnesses, symptoms, and treatment gaps
  • Research Scraping: Searches verified medical databases (PubMed, NEJM, JAMA, etc.) for relevant studies
  • Evidence Synthesis: Combines multiple research findings to create evidence-based recommendations
